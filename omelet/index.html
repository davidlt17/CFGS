Simple Omelette Recipe


An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese

Preparation Time

  • Total Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation 5 minutes
  • Cooking 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional filings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs.

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs
  2. Heat the pan
  3. Cook the omelette
  4. Add fillings (optional)
  5. Fold and serve
  6. Enjoy
